Meet

Christine

I meet all types of people. Any time I invite them to come to my classes, nine times out of ten, they respond by making jokes about their own bodies being unable to do yoga. “I am not flexible enough to do yoga” is the most common response that I get. The depictions of yoga in mainstream media have caused mass misconception and, sadly, many folks have also been traumatized by difficult fitness-based classes taught by unwelcoming yoga teachers, unprepared or unwilling to teach beginners. I had these “misconceptions” myself. I put the word in quotations because they have been and continue to be reinforced by yoga culture, but the yoga I wish to share is truly for everyone.
